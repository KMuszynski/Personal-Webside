SwiftBoard

First place!

Together with my 3 good friends,
we entered a <HAcK NA ZDROWIE 2> hackathon, where we developed SwiftBoard, a platform harnessing the power of AI to streamline the onboarding process.

To our delight our team secured the first place at the competition and won the grand prize!

To achieve this, we created a web application using TypeScript and React, and seamlessly connected it to a SupaBase backend.
In addition, we developed a chatbot integrated with the Chat GPT API, empowering it to provide virtual assistance.
Our chatbot featured pdf and callendar recognition capabilities, enabling it to offer support based on the displayed content.

Currently, we're actively expanding SwiftBoard's capabilities, with plans to develop a minimum viable product (MVP) and conduct field tests in selected companies.
